Welcome to Binyuan Capital

Binyuan Capital is a Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission licensed company that is also registered as investment advisor with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.

The core team members of Bin Yuan Capital comprises the original team from GE Asset Management (‘GEAM’) led by Ping Zhou. During Ping’s 17 years tenure at GEAM, most recently as Senior Vice President and Greater China Managing Director, the team managed over US$5bn across three funds – GE Emerging Market Strategy, China H Share Strategy, and China A Share Strategy. China H Share Strategy achieved an annualized return of 24% over 8.5 years ending 2010 while China A Share Strategy produced 37% (NB 30% outperformance vs. the benchmark) in cumulative returns since its inception in April 2007.

The fund invests in public equities, and deploys an active, disciplined research-driven process with a time-tested proprietary stock selection model. The investment model combines both quantitative and qualitative analysis underpinned by in-depth fundamental analysis. Our bottom-up approach includes, but not limited to the implementation of the following methods: company visits, network and channel checks, financial statement analysis, industry value chain analysis and the Porter Model.

Bin Yuan Capital has a strong commitment to compliance and risk control. The company adheres to the highest degree of investment and regulatory standards. Finally, all investment professionalism working for the fund/funds are required to be a CFA charter holder.
